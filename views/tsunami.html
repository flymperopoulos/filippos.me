Human Factors Interface Design

Human Factors Interface Design (HFID) is a class that focuses on interface design in modern technology. My team and I are currently working on a product that we hope can change our everyday "waking-up" experience.

From simple drawings, to user interviews, wireframes and UI mockups our team is working hard to truly understand the people we are designing this sproduct for; deeply comprehend their underlying motivations, needs and values, to ultimately build the best possible product. The class is currently in progress, so keep visiting the website for more updates!
