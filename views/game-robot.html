Principles of Engineering

During the fall semester of my sophomore year, 4 other Olin students and I decided to build a robotic gantry that independently plays the game of checkers against humans, as our final project for the Principles of Engineering class.

POE is a class that teaches students how to use microcontrollers in order to develop intricate systems with several subsystems, i.e. mechanical, electrical and software.


In the pictures shown above a CAD model of the system is provided, as well as a gif of how our Raspberry Pi camera detected contours using OpenCV.
